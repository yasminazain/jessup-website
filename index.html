About the Competition

What is the Jessup?

The Philip C. Jessup International Law Moot Court Competition is the world's largest moot court competition, bringing together law students from over 100 countries to argue a fictional dispute before a simulated International Court of Justice.

Teams research, write, and orally argue both sides of a complex public international law case — honing legal research, writing, and advocacy skills that matter on the global stage.

USK's Jessup Club represents Fakultas Hukum Universitas Syiah Kuala at the Indonesian National Round and beyond.
